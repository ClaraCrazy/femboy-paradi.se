Server Games

Welcome to the Server Games Hub! Here, you can find a variety of interactive games to play with fellow members of the community. Whether you want to strategize, joke around, or conquer the world, there's something for everyone!

🃏 Cards Against Humanity (CAH)

Enjoy the classic, dark-humored card game Cards Against Humanity with your server friends! Match hilarious responses to prompts and see who has the best (or worst) sense of humor.

  • Start a game: m!cah
  • List all available packs: m!cah list

👉 Be prepared for laughter, absurdity, and total chaos!

🗺️ Risk - Dominate the World!

In Risk, you battle for territory, expand your empire, and aim to rule the server’s battlefield. This strategy-based game requires cunning moves, alliances, and a thirst for victory!

  • Start a game: m!risk
  • View the map: m!risk map

👉 Are you ready to become the ultimate conqueror?

🏦 Monopoly - The Ultimate Business Tycoon Game

Buy, sell, and trade your way to riches in Monopoly! This classic game of strategy and luck lets you experience the thrill of owning it all.

  • Start a game: m!monopoly

👉 Bankrupt your friends and dominate the economy!

🔪 Mafia - Deception & Strategy

The Mafia game is a thrilling social deduction game where players take on different roles. The goal? Eliminate the Mafia or deceive everyone and take over!

  • Start a game: m!mafia
  • Get game info: m!mafia explain

👉 Will you survive the night, or will you be outwitted?
