MechaMew - Our Custom Bot

MechaMew is our powerful, custom-built bot that helps manage the server and adds fun features. It can be invoked using:

  • /mechamew
  • m!command
  • @MechaMew command

Key Commands:

  • m!staff - Notifies staff members.
  • m!profile (c!lvl) - View your leveling progress.
  • m!lvlset / c!setprofile / c!mypf - Customize your profile.
  • m!guildstats graphic - Generate server statistics.
