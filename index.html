Welcome to Femboy Paradise!

Step into a warm and inclusive space where femboys, admirers, and friends come together to chat, share, and have fun! Our server is built on a foundation of positivity, self-expression, and community. Whether you're looking to make friends, share your favorite outfits, or just chill, this is the place for you.

Our space is built on love and positivity. No matter who you are, you belong here. This is a place where labels don’t define us, and everyone is free to express themselves however they like. From lighthearted banter to deep conversations, every voice matters, and every person is valued. 🌈💬

And of course, we know that life isn’t always easy. That’s why Femboy Paradise is also a space for support and kindness. Whether you need a pick-me-up, some friendly advice, or just a comfy place to relax, there are always people here who care about you. You're never alone. 💗

🎭 Roles & Progression 🎭

Our server has a unique role system that rewards activity and engagement. Here’s how it works:

  • Unverified - For users who have not yet verified.
  • Blushy Babe - Verified members of the community.
  • Beloved Booster - Our server boosters.
  • Cherished Cutie - Trusted members, level 5 and above.
  • Peachy Prince - Our dedicated moderators.
  • Satin Sweetheart - Verification managers.
  • Dreamy Developer - The team behind our custom bot, MechaMew.
  • Admirable Admin - Server admins ensuring smooth operations.
  • Ethereal Cutie - Our custom bot that manages the entire server.
  • Ambiguous Alert - Temporary marker for suspicious users.

🤖 Meet MechaMew – Our Custom Bot! 🤖

MechaMew is our dedicated bot, bringing utility, moderation, and entertainment all in one. She helps keep things organized and fun with features like:

  • Moderation: Automatic rule enforcement, logging, and security features.
  • Leveling System: Gain XP and level up just by chatting! Use m!profile or m!lvl to check your progress.
  • Games: Play Monopoly, Mafia, Cards Against Humanity, and more!
  • Server Stats: View live stats with m!guildstats graphic.
  • Customization: Personalize your profile using m!lvlset, m!setprofile, or m!mypf.
