Welcome to Femboy Paradise!

Step into a warm and inclusive space where femboys, admirers, and friends come together to chat, share, and have fun! Our server is built on a foundation of positivity, self-expression, and community. Whether you're looking to make friends, share your favorite outfits, or just chill, this is the place for you.

🌸 What We Offer 🌸

  • A Welcoming Community: We celebrate femboys and their supporters! Everyone is welcome, as long as you respect the rules and each other.
  • Social Center: A place to share your selfies, introduce yourself, find friends, and even vent when needed.
  • Style Guides: Discuss and showcase your favorite fashion styles, get tips, and inspire others!
  • Fun and Games: We offer multiple server-integrated games like Monopoly, Risk, Mafia, and Cards Against Humanity!
  • Custom Bot – MechaMew: Our custom-built bot helps manage the server while providing entertainment, utility commands, and interactive features.

🎭 Roles & Progression 🎭

Our server has a unique role system that rewards activity and engagement. Here’s how it works:

  • 🎀 Unverified: New members start here with limited access. Verify yourself to unlock the full experience!
  • 💖 Blushy Babe: Our verified members get access to the server, joining the fun in all public channels.
  • ✨ Cherished Cutie: Trusted members who reach Level 5 gain access to the Social Center.
  • 👑 Peachy Prince: Our lovely moderators ensure the server stays a fun and safe place.
  • 💎 Satin Sweetheart: Verification managers who oversee new member verification.
  • 🛠️ Dreamy Developer: The team behind our custom bot, MechaMew.
  • 🚀 Admirable Admin: The ones handling server management and major decisions.
  • 🌟 Ethereal Enigma: Our beloved bot, MechaMew, making everything run smoothly.

🤖 Meet MechaMew – Our Custom Bot! 🤖

MechaMew is our dedicated bot, bringing utility, moderation, and entertainment all in one. She helps keep things organized and fun with features like:

  • Moderation: Automatic rule enforcement, logging, and security features.
  • Leveling System: Gain XP and level up just by chatting! Use m!profile or m!lvl to check your progress.
  • Games: Play Monopoly, Mafia, Cards Against Humanity, and more!
  • Server Stats: View live stats with m!guildstats graphic.
  • Customization: Personalize your profile using m!lvlset, m!setprofile, or m!mypf.
